Now Playing

See what the DJ is playing right now with the Now Playing tab!
  • Like or dislike a song with the thumbs up and down buttons to let the DJ know what you think of it.

  • If you are a DJ, then DJFeed's computer software lets you aggregate the data to form charts displaying which songs are hot and which are not.

Previous Songs

Too hung over to remember that amazing song that was played last night? Not to worry, Previous Songs lets you see the songs that were played in the order they were played.
  • See what others think of songs that were played.

  • Future implementation: Click on a song for the option of downloading it right to your phone for listening.

Voting

Let the DJ know what you want to hear!
  • Provides a list of songs the DJ is planning to play and allows you to vote for ones you want to hear the most.

  • DJFeed PC software collects the data and generates charts on the fly for DJ viewing.

  • If no DJ is present, DJFeed PC software dynamically updates the current playlist to include voted songs, allowing everyone to enjoy the music uninterrupted.

DJ's playlist

DJs - import playlist directly from your iTunes and edit it as you want.
  • Manage the playlist it on the fly, and it gets automatically reflected on iTunes.

  • People's vote counts are updated real time, and DJ can change the order of the songs to satisfy the audiences

Feedback Chart

DJs - Wanna check which songs was liked the most by people? Here is the way.
  • For all songs that had been played, chart shows the number of people who think its hot.

Request Songs

You can request a song to the DJ at the party!
  • People can either manually input the info of the song or just simply select the song on his phone to send the request.

  • Future Implementation : Uploading the song to DJ's server directly so that DJ can play the song even if he doesn't have the song.
